Panera Soupe

Description

This fabulous cinnamon-raisin French toast recipe should be prepared the night before baking. It's always a hit with friends and family and is best served with something salty on the side such as bacon or sausage. Enjoy! Can also be topped off with real maple syrup for an extra sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flopur
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 1/2 cups coarsley chopped broccoli florets
  • 1 cup matchstick-cut carrots
  • 1 stalk celery, thinly sliced
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat eggs, milk, and vanilla extract together in a medium mixing bowl and pour over bread. Cover the dish with foil and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight.
  3. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion; cook and stir until tender and translucent, about 5 minutes. Set aside.
  4. Whisk 1/4 cup melted butter and flour together in a large saucepan over medium-low heat. Continue to whisk and cook, adding 1 to 2 tablespoons of milk if necessary to keep the flour from burning, 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Gradually add milk while whisking constantly; stir in chicken stock and bring to a simmer.
  6. Cook until mixture is thickened, about 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Add broccoli, carrots, celery, and saut√©ed onion; simmer until all the vegetables are tender, about 10 to 15 minutes.
  8. Stir in Cheddar cheese until melted; season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.
